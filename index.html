About Flory Ordace

I'm Flory — a passionate content creator, model, and certified makeup artist based in the UK. With a flair for beauty, confidence, and creativity, I bring visions to life through storytelling, bold visuals, and authentic connection.

From high-glam looks to effortless everyday vibes, I create content that empowers and inspires — whether it's in front of the camera, behind the brush, or sharing real moments with my community. I've collaborated with lifestyle, fashion, skincare, and wellness brands to showcase their message in a way that feels both stylish and personal.

Whether you're here for beauty tips, brand collaborations, or a dose of everyday glow — welcome to my world.
